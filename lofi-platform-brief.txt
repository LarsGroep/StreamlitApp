LOFI ARTIST INTELLIGENCE PLATFORM
Product Vision & Development Brief
Received: 2026-06-12
=============================================

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The platform is a proprietary intelligence system — NOT a database.
Combines: Data Intelligence, Human Intelligence, Scene Intelligence, Market Intelligence, Booking Intelligence.
Ultimate objective: identify future opportunities before they become obvious to the broader market.

TWO KEY QUESTIONS THE PLATFORM ANSWERS
1. Who should we be paying attention to?
2. Can this artist actually sell tickets?

ARTIST SCOPE
- Emerging: detect 6-18 months before wider market, future headliners, breakout potential
- Established: evaluate demand, ticket-selling potential Amsterdam, momentum, fee justification

=============================================================================
PRIORITY 1A — CORRECT DATA FROM API AND OTHER SOURCES FOR ML MODELS
=============================================================================

Data Sources to integrate:
  Spotify, SoundCloud, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube
  Resident Advisor, Beatport
  Festival lineups, Club lineups
  Labels, Playlist placements, Event announcements

Track:
  AUDIENCE METRICS: monthly listeners, followers, follower growth, engagement growth, geo growth
  ARTIST DEVELOPMENT: release performance, label associations, booking frequency, festival appearances,
                      headline appearances, support slots for larger artists
  INDUSTRY METRICS: follows/mentions from influential artists/promoters, label support,
                    festival support, agency representation

Goal: Single source of truth, clean historical datasets, data quality before model training

=============================================================================
PRIORITY 1B — AGENT INTELLIGENCE LAYER
=============================================================================

Output per artist:
  - Momentum Score
  - Growth Score
  - Market Relevance Score
  - Future Potential Score
  - Confidence Score

=============================================================================
PRIORITY 1C — GRADIENT BOOSTING ML MODEL (replace/augment Isolation Forest)
=============================================================================

Emerging artists predict:
  - Future growth potential
  - Breakout probability
  - Future headliner potential
  - Future booking demand
  - Future market value

Established artists predict:
  - Ticket-selling potential
  - Audience demand
  - Market momentum / growth vs decline
  - Future relevance
  - Resonance with LOFI audience

KEY PRINCIPLE: Growth acceleration > current size.
A smaller artist growing rapidly is more valuable than a larger stagnant artist.

=============================================================================
PRIORITY 2A — HUMAN FEEDBACK LOOP (ML updatable over time)
=============================================================================

Booking team can:
  - Track specific artists
  - Adjust momentum scores
  - Add qualitative feedback (buzz, crowd response, industry discussion, upcoming releases)
  - Compare emerging vs established within same sound
  - Define which metrics matter per genre

Qualitative feedback examples:
  - Big release coming up
  - Strong industry buzz
  - Strong crowd response during support slots
  - Stage was already full during opening set
  - Artist is being discussed heavily within the scene

Feedback categories:
  - Fits LOFI
  - Doesn't Fit LOFI
  - Sound We Would Like To Develop
  - Already Saturated
  - Interesting Support Act
  - Potential Future Headliner

- export_to_excel.py: multi-sheet Excel export
