LOFI Artist Intelligence Platform
Product Vision & Development Brief
________________


Executive Summary
The purpose of this platform is to give LOFI a sustainable competitive advantage in artist discovery, artist evaluation and booking decisions.
The platform should not function as a database.
The platform should function as a proprietary intelligence system that combines:
* Data Intelligence
* Human Intelligence
* Scene Intelligence
* Market Intelligence
* Booking Intelligence
The ultimate objective is to identify future opportunities before they become obvious to the broader market.
The system should support both:
Emerging Artists
* Detect artists 6–18 months before the wider market
* Identify future headliners early
* Surface artists with breakout potential
* Discover artists we would otherwise miss
Established Artists
* Evaluate audience demand before booking decisions
* Assess ticket-selling potential in Amsterdam
* Measure current momentum and relevance
* Identify whether an artist is growing, stable or declining
* Help determine whether an artist is worth the requested fee
The platform should answer two key questions:
1. Who should we be paying attention to?
2. Can this artist actually sell tickets?

Ultimate Goal
The goal is not to build a better artist database.
The goal is to build a proprietary intelligence system that helps LOFI:
* Discover artists before competitors
* Identify future headliners early
* Understand emerging scenes and sounds
* Make smarter booking decisions
* Predict audience demand
* Improve lineup construction
* Build a long-term competitive advantage
Success means that the platform consistently identifies future artists, scenes and trends before they become obvious to the wider market.
The ultimate measure of success is simple:
If the platform helps LOFI discover and book the next Chris Stussy, Josh Baker, Kolter, Rossi., Mau P or PAWSA before the rest of the market, it is succeeding.
